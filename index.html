Good to Know

Frequently asked

Are all parathas vegetarian?

Yes — our entire menu is 100% pure vegetarian. We cook only in vegetarian oils and pure desi ghee.

What comes with a stuffed paratha?

Every stuffed paratha is served with homemade curd (100 g), fresh mint chutney and mixed pickle. Butter on top is optional and free.

How do I place an order?

Order directly on WhatsApp for fastest service, or find us on Zomato and Swiggy. Combos and mix-and-match family packs are available on all channels.

What are your delivery hours?

We're open daily from 8:00 AM to 11:30 AM and again from 2:30 PM to 10:00 PM (offline during school hours in between). Breakfast parathas are available from open — perfect for a hot morning meal.

Do you cater for groups and offices?

Absolutely. Message us on WhatsApp with your headcount and we'll put together a bulk paratha platter with sides.

How do you keep parathas fresh on delivery?

Parathas are made to order, wrapped in butter paper to stay crisp, and sides are sealed separately so nothing goes soggy on the way.
